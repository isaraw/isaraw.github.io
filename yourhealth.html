    Integrated Digital Campaign for Cancer Awareness Charity | 2016

Your Health Your Choice

"Your Health Your Choice" is a disease awareness campaign created for a leading cancer information and awareness charity in Ireland. The campaign aimed to educate people on how they can prevent 4 in 10 cancers by making simple lifestyle changes: quit smoking, limit alcohol consumption, avoid excessive sun exposure, maintain a healthy diet, and stay physically active.

The Challenge

Our team and the charity developed a comprehensive, multi-channel digital campaign to:

• Drive traffic to the microsite
• Increase awareness and educate people about key lifestyle changes to reduce cancer risk using video and infographics

The primary audience was the general public, with a focus on cancer survivors and young adults.

Strategy

We implemented a range of digital activities, including a microsite, an animated video, Google Search and Banner Ads, Social Media Marketing (Twitter and Facebook ads), Search Engine Optimization, Affiliate marketing, and Email Marketing.

The microsite featured health tips on quitting smoking, sun protection, and reducing alcohol intake. We also created a downloadable infographic and a video. All interactions, such as PDF downloads and video views, were tracked using Google Analytics.

Results

The campaign exceeded the key metrics defined. Over 800,000 targeted display ad impressions resulted in a Click Through Rate (CTR) of 0.11% and a conversion rate of 10.5% on the microsite. The campaign effectively drove significant traffic, educated the audience, and increased awareness about reducing cancer risk through lifestyle changes.
