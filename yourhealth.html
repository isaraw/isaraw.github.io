    Integrated Digital Campaign for Cancer Awareness Charity

Your Health Your Choice 

The Your Health Your Choice is a disease awareness campaign created for a leading cancer information and awareness Irish charity. The aim of  ‘Your Health Your Choice campaign’ was to make people aware that they can prevent 4 in 10 cancers if they made simple changes to their everyday lives: quit smoking; limit how much alcohol you drink; avoid too much sun exposure; eat a healthy diet and be a healthy weight; be physically active

The Challenge

Combined Media and the charity created a fully integrated, multi-channel digital campaign to:

drive traffic to the microsite
increase awareness and educate people about the importance of lifestyle factors, specifically four key cancer risk reduction preventions with the use of video and infographics

The main target audience was the general public with a focus on cancer survivors and young adults

Strategy

A range of marketing activities included; a microsite, video, Google Search and Banner Ads, Social Media Marketing, including Twitter and Facebook ads, Search Engine Optimization,  Affiliate marketing and Email Marketing.

The microsite was created which contained information about health choices ranging from quitting smoking to wearing adequate sun screen to reducing the amount of alcohol you drink. A downloadable infographic and a video were also create and made available on the site. All events were tracked including PDF downloads and video views.

SOCIAL MEDIA MARKETING

Facebook and Twitter website clicks and Video ads were created targeting Irish Males and Females from 18 to 65+ and Cancer pages and associations followers

SEARCH ENGINE MARKETING

Google Adwords Search and Display campaign were set up. The Google Search campaign in particular was set up on the charity Google Grant's account. The website was optimized for search engines

Implementation

Banner advertising was displayed across female network including; Marie Claire, Celebs Now and Look magazine and male online magazines such as Golfnet, hoganstand.com/hurling, and irishrugby.ie

An email shot was sent out to the database of emails on file. Social Media campaigns were created across various platforms. A customised Facebook tab was introduced which acted similar to a mini-website on Facebook.

AFFILIATE MARKETING

Banner Ads were promoted across a network of Female and Male websites, including Marie Claire, InStyle, Now Magazine, Look, Digital Spy, irishrugby.ie, hoganstand.com, Golfnet etc. Banner ads and native content were also promoted on Campus.ie, targeting a younger audience

EMAIL MARKETING

A follow up email was sent to the charity's email list of users
